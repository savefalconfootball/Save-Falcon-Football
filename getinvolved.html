How to help


The primary way to help is to voice your concern. In the form below, you can fill out information about you and leave a comment about what Bowling Green football means to you.
Your name will be sent to the university administration along with all others who have voiced their concern about the football program. Your comments may appear anonymously on this webpage.
Additionally, you can pledge an amount to give should the opportunity to buy out the contract arise. It would cost approximately $800,000 to buy out Mike Jinks' contract. If you pledge an amount, it should be something you are able to give if asked.


* Required
